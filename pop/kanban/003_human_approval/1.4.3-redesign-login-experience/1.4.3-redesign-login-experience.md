# 1.4.3-redesign-login-experience — Redesign the login experience

- **Phase:** [[roadmap/1-administrative-foundation|Phase 1.4]]
- **Plan:** [[1.4.3-redesign-login-experience.plan]] · **Approval:** [[1.4.3-redesign-login-experience.approval]] · **Verification:** [[1.4.3-redesign-login-experience.verify]]

## What

Recompose `/login` with the approved shared shadcn system as a professional bilingual authentication experience. Deliver intentional hierarchy and copy, accessible labels and generic errors, complete pending/disabled/focus/recovery states, responsive composition, light/dark behavior, and browser visual evidence without changing the authentication contract.

## Why

Login is the product's first trust boundary and currently appears as an uncomposed HTML form that neither demonstrates nor benefits from the shared design system.
