## What

Define six stable named theme personalities with at least two light and two dark variants, semantic tokens, typography, motion, contrast evidence, deterministic fallback behavior, and an audit of the project UI skills with targeted improvements only if they would force rigid or generic execution.

## Why

All panel, storefront, and checkout redesigns need one lintable visual contract before feature-specific styling begins.
