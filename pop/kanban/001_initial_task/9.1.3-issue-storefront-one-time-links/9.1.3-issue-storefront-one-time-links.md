# 9.1.3-issue-storefront-one-time-links — Issue storefront one-time links

- **Phase:** [[roadmap/9-public-storefront-and-checkout|Phase 9.1]]
- **Plan:** [[9.1.3-issue-storefront-one-time-links.plan]] · **Approval:** [[9.1.3-issue-storefront-one-time-links.approval]] · **Verification:** [[9.1.3-issue-storefront-one-time-links.verify]]

## What

Add a separately rate-limited sessionless cart command that revalidates store, owner, products, quantities, prices, currency, limits, and totals before issuing one owner-derived one-time link, then clears only that store's cart and redirects only after successful issuance.

## Why

Browser carts are untrusted and must never choose payable facts or gain the merchant's authenticated creation authority.
