# 7.1.2-extend-merchant-store-settings — Extend merchant store settings

## What

Persist each merchant's theme, logo reference, boxed/table layout, store and standalone-payment toggles, slug, display name, and one active default currency with deterministic fallbacks, including the exact standalone-payment-only behavior when the catalog storefront is disabled.

## Why

Public storefront and checkout behavior cannot be derived safely until the complete owner-controlled store contract exists.
