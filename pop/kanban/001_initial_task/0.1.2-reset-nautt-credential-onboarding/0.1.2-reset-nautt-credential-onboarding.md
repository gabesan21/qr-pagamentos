## O quê

Give an authenticated owner an explicit, safe reset of a failed or ambiguous Nautt credential/webhook onboarding (states `REGISTERING`/`INDETERMINATE`) back to a retryable state, with bilingual UI affordance and redacted outcomes.

## Por quê

A real onboarding left the owner locked: provider unreachable during `POST /client-webhooks` made the result ambiguous, and the app intentionally blocks retry/replacement with no recovery path — the user cannot do anything.
