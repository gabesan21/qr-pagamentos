# 2.3.1-verify-and-handle-nautt-webhooks — Verify and handle Nautt webhooks

## What

Verify raw request bytes with HMAC-SHA256 and constant-time comparison, deduplicate `X-Nautt-Delivery`, acknowledge within 15 seconds, and reconcile each event through an authoritative provider-order read.

## Why

Provider events can change payment state only after the application proves their authenticity and converges through owner-scoped order data.
