# Approval — [[3.2.2-product-public-read-boundary]]

## Round 1 — 2026-07-20

### Decision brief

- **Delivery:** A server-only, product-scoped read model for later checkout consumers.
- **Boundary:** Active products only; callers receive either `null` or exactly the localized `title`, `description`, and canonical-string `price`.
- **Exclusions:** No HTTP/UI/dictionary/payment-link surface, identifier or administrative-data leakage, currency addition, or numeric price conversion.
- **Topology:** One owned implementation-and-test front under `src/auth/`.
- **Plan:** [[3.2.2-product-public-read-boundary.plan]] — *follow for the complete execution brief*.

The approved contract is exact and proportionate: an uncached database predicate
enforces active-only visibility; malformed, absent, and inactive records collapse
to the same `null`; the closed locale input selects only the corresponding
persisted public fields; and the explicit DTO/select prevent administrative or
provider leakage. The plan keeps price as the canonical string and excludes every
premature HTTP, UI, checkout, currency, migration, or data-model expansion. Its
single front has complete, non-overlapping ownership and objective focused and
aggregate re-run gates.
