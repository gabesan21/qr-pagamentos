# 4.1.1-owner-isolation-and-checkout-policy — Owner isolation and checkout policy

- **Phase:** [[roadmap/4-checkout-and-orders|Phase 4.1]]
- **Plan:** [[4.1.1-owner-isolation-and-checkout-policy.plan]] · **Approval:** [[4.1.1-owner-isolation-and-checkout-policy.approval]] · **Verification:** [[4.1.1-owner-isolation-and-checkout-policy.verify]]

## What

Require one owner for every product and payment link, enforce account isolation, and let each account configure the data requested by its public checkout with `NONE` as the default.

## Why

Public checkout and order lifecycle must derive one trusted owner and policy before any customer payment can be created.
