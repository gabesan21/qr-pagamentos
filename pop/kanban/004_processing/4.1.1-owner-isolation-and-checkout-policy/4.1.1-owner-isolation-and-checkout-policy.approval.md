# Approval — [[4.1.1-owner-isolation-and-checkout-policy]]

## Round 1 — 2026-07-21

### Decision brief

- **Delivery:** Required persisted owner isolation for products/payment links and one account checkout-data policy with `NONE` default.
- **Boundary:** A link's persisted owner must equal its product owner; owner-scoped services/routes never trust browser identities. `/admin` remains global administration/catalog only, while each authenticated account manages its own products, links, and policy.
- **Exclusions:** No public resolver change, checkout/order/customer-data submission, provider call, price coercion, single-use consumption, or hosted payment link.
- **Topology:** Sequential persistence → owner-server-boundary → authenticated-owner-surface fronts with non-overlapping ownership.
- **Verification:** `pnpm db:generate`, `pnpm db:contract-check`, focused unit/route/static-render suites, and `pnpm check`. Docker, `pnpm db:test`, and browser evidence are explicitly deferred by user authorization.
- **Spec proposal (returned):** The former plan deferred the same-owner persistence and catalog role-boundary wording to 006; round 2 places that wording in the linked specs before renewed 003 review.
- **Plan:** [[4.1.1-owner-isolation-and-checkout-policy.plan]] — *follow for the complete execution brief*.

### Decision brief

- **Durable contract:** The linked specs now expressly require each payment link to share its persisted owner with its referenced product; persistence and server boundaries reject a mismatch.
