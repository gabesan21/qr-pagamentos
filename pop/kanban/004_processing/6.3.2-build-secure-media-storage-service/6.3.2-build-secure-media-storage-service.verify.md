## Blocking finding

### B1 — The filesystem probe accepts semantics it claims to verify

`src/media/media-storage.ts:120-139` creates a random absent source, opens it
with `O_EXCL|O_NOFOLLOW`, links it once to an absent target, and successfully
opens that ordinary target. Those successful operations do not prove:

- `O_EXCL` rejects an already-existing source;
- hard-link publication rejects an already-existing final target without
  replacing it; or
- `O_NOFOLLOW` rejects a symlink at the opened path.

The approved plan, `pop/specs/media-storage.md`, and `src/media/AGENTS.md`
explicitly make those fail-closed probe guarantees a precondition for accepting
the filesystem. The separate publication tests at
`src/media/media-storage.test.ts:67-92` demonstrate current-host Node behavior,
but they do not make `initialize()` reject a mounted filesystem whose semantics
are incompatible at runtime.

**Required correction:** extend the private initialization probe with
collision and symlink fixtures that require the expected failure codes and
verify the original target remains unchanged; keep cleanup and both directory
syncs fail-closed. Add initialization tests that inject or simulate each
unsupported semantic and prove initialization refuses the storage root.
