# Plan — [[6.1.2-enforce-role-exclusive-authentication]]

- **Stage:** 002_planning · **Responsible:** planning agent

## Objective and expected result

- **Objective:** implement the approved mutually exclusive `ADMIN`/merchant `USER` capability matrix at authentication, page/route, and owner-service boundaries.
- **Observable result:** successful login continues through `/`; an active `ADMIN` reaches `/admin` without merchant work, an active `USER` reaches the merchant home, unauthenticated callers keep existing `401`/login outcomes, and administrators receive `403` or `/admin` before any merchant read, parsing, mutation, provider operation, or owner-scoped persistence.

## Strategy

Use [[6.1.1-define-panel-architecture-and-permissions]] as the fixed dependency contract: it is integrated at commit `4a8e09af98783103d2cefcc33da722f03462cfd8`. Add one true merchant-only authorization primitive with the same active-session resolution semantics as the admin primitive: no active principal is unauthenticated; an active wrong-role `ADMIN` is forbidden. Apply it before merchant work at cookie guards and pages, and independently at every principal-taking owner service so bypassing a route cannot grant an administrator merchant capability.

Keep `/login/submit` dispatching successful authentication through `/`; make `/` choose the role before locale, Nautt, catalog, payment-link, checkout-policy, storefront, or order work. Preserve the approved administrator-only global order projection and its redacted read-only methods. Retire `/admin/users/[id]/nautt-credentials` to denial-only without parsing input or invoking a service. Do not change public/sessionless routes, identifiers, opaque outcomes, locale stubs, provider protocols, or data models.

## Affected areas

- `src/auth/authorization*`, `src/app/owner-guard*`, `src/app/page*`, `src/app/admin-access/` — role primitives, cookie guard, and role landing.
- Merchant services in `src/auth/`, `src/orders/order-view*`, and `src/integrations/nautt/owner-onboarding*` — defense-in-depth role denial.
- Merchant pages and POST routes under `src/app/{orders,products,payment-links,checkout-policy,nautt-credentials,storefront}/` — deny before reads, parsing, or work.
- `src/app/admin/users/[id]/nautt-credentials/` — denial-only compatibility endpoint.
- `AGENTS.md` and `src/integrations/nautt/AGENTS.md` — DOX review for changed ownership/authorization boundaries.

## Risks and abort conditions

- **Risk:** route-only protection leaves callable owner services open — mitigate with service tests proving zero downstream work for `ADMIN`.
- **Risk:** broad role denial accidentally removes approved admin global reads — mitigate with explicit `listForAdmin`/`getForAdmin` and `/admin/orders*` regression runs.
- **Risk:** denial occurs after form parsing, decryption, or provider/store calls — require zero-call assertions at each boundary.
- **Abort if:** enforcing exclusivity requires changing a public/sessionless contract, persistence model, or provider lifecycle; mark `blocked: true` with the conflicting path and return to 002.
