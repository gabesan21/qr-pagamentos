## Finding

1. **Blocking — `/admin-access` changes the established disabled-session outcome and conflicts with the active-principal model.** `pop/specs/administrative-foundation.md:50,64` requires an active cookie principal for protected access but assigns empty `403` to a disabled principal; `AGENTS.md:88` repeats `403 otherwise`. In the current durable authorization boundary, `src/auth/authorization.ts:18-27` resolves a disabled account to `null`, so `requireAdmin` raises `UnauthenticatedError`, and `src/app/admin-access/route.ts:7-12` returns empty `401`. This contradicts the plan's preserved protected outcomes and would require role enforcement either to weaken the active-principal boundary or silently change an existing compatibility status. Classify disabled/revoked sessions consistently as unauthenticated `401` (leaving `403` for an active wrong-role `USER`) in both spec and DOX, or explicitly justify and contract the broader authentication change.
